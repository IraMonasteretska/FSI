You Have The Right to Mental Health

Recognizing you need support for your mental health isn’t always easy, and reaching out can be just as difficult. We’re here so you don’t have to go through this alone.

What to Expect

Quality Support:

Our services are grounded in evidence-based practices that honor your values, culture and lived experiences through personalized care planning.

Wellness + Options:

You have access to a range of services, including individual therapy, group therapy, medication and medical management, and collaborative care with other providers in our community.

Affordable,
Personalized Care:

Our services are available to anyone in need of support, regardless of insurance status or income level, with a sliding fee schedule for qualifying individuals. Many of our services are also available through telehealth if that’s the best option for your care.

Support for the
Whole Family:

Children and teens often need specialized support to develop their social and emotional skills and cope with their life experiences. Learn more about our children’s mental health services and family therapy options here.
