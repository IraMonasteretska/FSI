Welcome

Welcome to the Family Partnership Center, where you will find over 25 community organizations to fit your needs and interests. Known by the Poughkeepsie community as “The Partnership” or “The Ship,” this building is a valued community center where people of all ages can access core services, athletic and arts programs, cultural and educational opportunities, and more!

Owned and operated by Family Services, the Family Partnership Center emphasizes the power of collaboration through co-location. Our partners easily refer you to other services within the same building, coordinating efforts to ensure responsiveness and access.
